Pick Up

Big Brothers Big Sisters Foundation helps provide mentors to children in need by picking up your used clothes, and shoes, and sells them. 100% of their profits go back into the communities they are helping, nothing is kept by the staff. You can find them at bbbsfoundation.org, or call using one of two available phone numbers: (774) 776-7200 or (800) 483-5503.

Share Your Soles takes in people's gently used or new shoes to give people in impoverished counties shoes, since they can not afford them. You can read more on shareyoursoles.org or call them at (708) 448-4469.

The Salvation Army is a nonprofit organization that aims to help rehabilitation centers for people recovering from addictions, as well as help families and people in need. In order to donate you can go to salvationarmyusa.com or call 1-800-SAL-ARMY.

Drop Out/Ship Out

Goodwill is an organization that employs people that have a hard time finding or getting jobs, by donating, you are enabling people to obtain jobs while also getting rid of clothing that other people may then be able to use. You can donate by dropping off clothing at one of their stores, or clothes bins, that can be found at goodwill.org.

WGirls is an organization that helps empower women, one way they have helped a lot is through providing prom dresses to young girls. By going to wgirls.org you can assist by donating your own used dresses as long at they meet the guide line.
